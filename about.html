Our story

Your Trusted Local Auto Repair Shop in Dallas

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our customers safe and their vehicles reliable. We believe in transparency, integrity, and exceptional craftsmanship.

Ruben's Auto Repair was founded with a simple mission: offer Dallas drivers a better auto repair experience. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, Ruben saw too many shops prioritizing profit over people. He built his own shop on the principles of honesty, quality, and community. Every technician is trained to treat customers like neighbors and cars like their own.

Today, Ruben's Auto Repair serves hundreds of satisfied customers across Dallas. We've earned a reputation for fair pricing, clear communication, and reliable repairs. Whether it's a quick oil change or a complex engine rebuild, we approach every job with the same dedication. We're proud to be a part of the Dallas community and look forward to earning your trust.
