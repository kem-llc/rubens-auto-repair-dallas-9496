Our story

Dallas's Reliable Auto Repair Shop Since 2008

At Ruben's Auto Repair, our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe on the road. We believe in transparency, integrity, and exceptional customer service.

Ruben's Auto Repair started as a small garage in East Dallas with a simple philosophy: treat every customer like family. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service auto repair shop trusted by thousands of Dallas drivers. Our team of ASE-certified technicians brings decades of combined experience, and we invest in the latest diagnostic equipment to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs.

We're proud to be a locally owned and operated business that gives back to the community. Whether you're a long-time resident or new to the area, you can count on Ruben's Auto Repair for honest advice, fair prices, and quality workmanship. We don't just fix cars—we build relationships.
