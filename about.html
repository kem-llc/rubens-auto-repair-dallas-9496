Our story

Dallas's Trusted Auto Repair Since 1985

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair that keeps our community safe on the road – with transparent pricing and a smile.

Ruben's Auto Repair was founded in 1985 by Ruben Martinez, a master mechanic with a simple philosophy: treat every car like it belongs to your mother. Starting in a small two-bay shop on Peak Street, Ruben built a reputation for integrity and skilled work. Over the decades, the business has grown but never lost its family feel. Today, Ruben's son Carlos leads a team of ASE-certified technicians who share that same commitment to excellence.

We've served generations of Dallas families, from Deep Ellum to Lakewood and beyond. Our shop has expanded to handle modern vehicles with advanced electronics, but our core values remain unchanged: fair pricing, quality parts, and standing behind every repair. When you bring your car to Ruben's, you're not just a customer – you're part of our family.
