Brake Repair & Replacement

Restore stopping power with professional brake inspections and repairs.

Schedule this service

Overview

Your brakes are your vehicle's most important safety system. Our technicians perform thorough brake inspections, measuring pad thickness, rotor condition, and fluid levels. We replace worn pads and rotors, resurface drums, and flush brake fluid to keep your stopping distance short and safe.

What's included

  • Enhanced safety with reliable stopping power
  • Reduced noise and vibration from worn components
  • Extended brake system life with proactive maintenance

How it works

1

Inspect Brake System

We remove wheels and inspect pads, rotors, calipers, and lines for wear or damage.

2

Replace Worn Parts

Install new brake pads and rotors or drums as needed, using quality components.

3

Test & Adjust

Reassemble, test brake feel, and perform a road test to ensure proper performance.

Common questions

How do I know if my brakes need service?

Common signs include squeaking or grinding noises, a soft brake pedal, vibration when braking, or a dashboard warning light.

How long do brake pads last?

Brake pad lifespan varies by driving habits and conditions, typically between 30,000 and 60,000 miles.
