Overview

Your brakes are your vehicle's most critical safety system. At Ruben's Auto Repair, we provide comprehensive brake inspections, repairs, and replacements. Whether you need new pads, rotors, calipers, or a complete brake system overhaul, our technicians use quality parts and precise techniques to restore stopping power and reliability.

What's included

  • Thorough inspection of all brake components
  • Use of premium pads and rotors for long life
  • No squeaks or vibrations – smooth stopping every time
  • Competitive pricing with warranty on parts and labor

How it works

1

Inspection

We check brake pads, rotors, calipers, lines, and fluid levels to identify any issues.

2

Repair/Replace

Worn or damaged components are replaced with high-quality parts, and the system is bled and adjusted.

3

Test Drive

We test the brakes at various speeds to ensure proper function and your safety.

Common questions

How often should I replace my brake pads?

Typically every 30,000 to 70,000 miles, but it depends on driving habits and conditions. We inspect them during every service.

What are signs my brakes need service?

Squealing, grinding, a soft brake pedal, or vibrations when braking are common indicators. Bring your car in for a free inspection if you notice any of these.
