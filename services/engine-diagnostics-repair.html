Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Advanced diagnostics to pinpoint and fix engine issues fast.

Schedule this service

Overview

When your check engine light comes on or your car isn't performing as it should, our advanced diagnostic tools can quickly identify the root cause. From sensor failures to major mechanical problems, we have the expertise to repair your engine and restore performance. We explain the issue clearly and provide options so you can make an informed decision.

What's included

  • State-of-the-art diagnostic scanners for accurate codes
  • Experienced technicians who interpret data correctly
  • Efficient repairs that save you time and money
  • Clear communication about what's needed and why

How it works

1

Scan & Analyze

We connect diagnostic equipment to read error codes and live data from your engine.

2

Inspect & Test

We perform visual and mechanical tests to confirm the issue and check for related problems.

3

Repair & Verify

We fix the problem, clear codes, and test drive to ensure everything is working perfectly.

Common questions

Why is my check engine light on?

It could be something simple like a loose gas cap, or more serious like an oxygen sensor or catalytic converter issue. We'll diagnose it accurately.

Can you diagnose a car that won't start?

Yes. We can test the battery, starter, alternator, and fuel system to determine why your car won't start and get you back on the road.
