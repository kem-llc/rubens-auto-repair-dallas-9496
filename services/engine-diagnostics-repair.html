Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Accurate diagnostics and expert repairs for check engine lights and performance issues.

Overview

When your check engine light comes on or your car isn't running right, our advanced diagnostic tools pinpoint the problem quickly. From sensor failures to major engine repairs, we have the expertise to restore your vehicle's performance and reliability.

What's included

  • Fast, accurate diagnosis saves time and money
  • Repairs performed by experienced, ASE-certified technicians
  • Clear explanation of issues and repair options before work begins

How it works

1

Scan & Retrieve Codes

Connect to your vehicle's computer to read diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs).

2

Perform Tests

Conduct additional tests like compression checks, fuel pressure tests, and vacuum tests to confirm the issue.

3

Repair & Verify

Complete the necessary repairs and clear codes, then test drive to confirm the fix.

Common questions

What does a check engine light mean?

It can indicate anything from a loose gas cap to a serious engine problem. We'll diagnose the exact cause.

Can I drive with the check engine light on?

If the light is steady, you can usually drive to our shop. If it's flashing, pull over and call us immediately.
