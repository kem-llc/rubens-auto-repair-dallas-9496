Tire Services & Alignment

Keep your tires in top shape with rotations, balancing, and alignments.

Schedule this service

Overview

Proper tire maintenance improves safety, fuel efficiency, and ride comfort. We offer tire rotation, balancing, and wheel alignment services to ensure even tread wear and stable handling. We also inspect tires for damage and recommend replacements when needed.

What's included

  • Extends tire life and improves fuel economy
  • Enhances vehicle stability and handling
  • Prevents uneven wear and premature replacement

How it works

1

Inspect Tires & Pressure

Check tire condition, tread depth, and adjust air pressure to manufacturer specs.

2

Rotate & Balance

Move tires to recommended positions and balance each wheel to eliminate vibrations.

3

Align Suspension

Adjust camber, caster, and toe angles to ensure straight tracking and even wear.

Common questions

How often should I rotate my tires?

Every 5,000 to 7,500 miles, typically with your oil change.

What are signs my car needs an alignment?

Your car pulls to one side, the steering wheel is off-center, or you notice uneven tire wear.
